About

Mythras Encounter Generator is a tool for generating random NPC's and monsters for Mythras / RuneQuest 6.

One of the fundamental principles of the tool is that the creation of the content — the NPC/monster specifications and templates — is created by the community. Anybody can create a new template, that other users can later use to generate NPCs/monsters.
