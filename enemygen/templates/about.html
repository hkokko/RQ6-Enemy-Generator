About

RuneQuest Encounter Generator is a tool for generating random NPC's and monsters for RuneQuest 6.

One of the fundamental principles of the tool is, that the creation of the content - the NPC/monsters specifications and templates - is created by the community. Anybody can create a new template, that other users can later use to generate NPC's/monsters.
